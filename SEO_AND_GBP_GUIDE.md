### Step 2: Image Alt Text
*   Go through [constants.tsx](file:///c:/Users/LENOVO/Downloads/dental%20clinic/constants.tsx).
*   Every time there is an image, ensure there is descriptive text.
*   *Code Example:* `<img src="..." alt="Patient receiving dental implant in Velachery clinic" />`
*   **Why?** Google Image Search brings local traffic.

### Step 3: Create a Sitemap
*   Use a free generator like [xml-sitemaps.com](https://www.xml-sitemaps.com/).
*   Enter the live URL (e.g., `oralcare-chennai.com`).
*   Download `sitemap.xml`.
*   Put it in your `public/` folder.
